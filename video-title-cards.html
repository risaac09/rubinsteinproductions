How to Use These Templates

These templates are designed for video overlays, YouTube thumbnails, and title cards. To get crisp, production-ready graphics:

  • Browser zoom to 200% (Cmd/Ctrl + Plus twice) to view the cards at full resolution
  • Take a screenshot at 200% zoom for a 3840×2160px image (2x resolution)
  • Export as PNG with transparent background by cropping to card boundaries
  • Use in video editor as overlays, lower thirds, or title card sequences
  • Cards are shown at 50% scale in this preview for easier browsing
