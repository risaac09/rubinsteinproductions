Evaluation that names the decision the report has to inform.

I lead evaluations for state agencies, foundations, and community coalitions running state- and federally-funded programs across health equity, traffic safety, behavioral health, and place-based initiatives. Engagements run mixed-methods — surveys, interviews, focus groups, and administrative data — and are scoped to the decision a named user is going to make with the findings.
