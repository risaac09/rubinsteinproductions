Most organizations are stuck between knowing what's true and saying it. The messaging sounds generic because the articulation happens downstream of lived experience, not inside it.

The gap isn't a communications problem. It's a safety problem — the institutional environment has made honesty feel expensive.

Say Why is RP's methodology for helping leaders and teams metabolize what they already know into expression that carries the weight of cost, body, and relation. Output: artifacts that travel without you in the room.

— the practice, in 60 words

the methodology

Say Why.

Facilitation grounded in nervous-system science. A camera that ships to you, not a crew that arrives. Measurement in two registers — institutional and relational. Everything returns.

01 · phase

Container

Relational safety and institutional permission, built simultaneously. Logic model. Roadmap the organization can sign off on.

02 · phase

Excavation

Surface the inside version. Map the feedback loops keeping honest expression contained. Recognition: that's it — that's the thing.

03 · phase

Performance

The camera ships. You film yourself, on your terms, in your space. I facilitate remotely. Zero-measurement zone — emergence happens because it isn't being measured.

04 · phase

Translation

A 3-minute brand video from your raw footage. All footage, data, and deliverables returned. Bilingual Dashboard for Program Engagement and above.
